# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to our project! We welcome contributions in the form of guides, videos, and documentation. Please follow these guidelines:

## Guidelines for Contributing Guides
- Ensure your guides are clear and concise.
- Use headings and bullet points for easier readability.
- Provide examples and screenshots where applicable.
- Submit your guides as pull requests for review.

## Guidelines for Contributing Videos
- Keep videos focused and to-the-point, ideally under 10 minutes.
- Use high-quality audio and video (1080p recommended).
- Provide a transcript of your video in your pull request.
- Include relevant tags and descriptions to enhance discoverability.

## Guidelines for Contributing Documentation
- Follow the existing documentation structure.
- Use markdown syntax for formatting.
- Ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date.
- Provide references or links to external resources when necessary.

We appreciate your contributions! Together, we can improve our project for everyone.
